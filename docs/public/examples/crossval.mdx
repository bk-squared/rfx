## Reading rule

- Use these scripts when you need a current runnable reference.
- Keep claims scoped to the workflow being exercised.
- Treat non-uniform paths as shadow unless a guide says otherwise; treat
  distributed, Floquet/Bloch, subgridding, generalized planar ports, and
  advanced inverse-design paths as experimental unless a guide says otherwise.
  Coaxial line-reflection claims must stay inside the documented one-port
  transmission-line envelope.
