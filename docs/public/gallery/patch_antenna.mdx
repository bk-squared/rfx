Probe-fed 2.4 GHz rectangular microstrip patch antenna on FR4 (εr = 4.3,
1.5 mm), driven by a 50 Ω lumped port. The patch resonance shows as a local
S11 dip near the Balanis transmission-line frequency.

<Aside type="note" title="Validation — tier E4">
**Metric:** resonance frequency within 20% of the OpenEMS Harminv resonance;
`|S11|` passive (`≤ 1.05`).
**Tolerance:** resonance-frequency error `< 20%`, `|S11| ≤ 1.05`.
**Reference solver:** OpenEMS Harminv (coarse-mesh resonance agreement — not a
calibrated absolute-S claim).
</Aside>

dielectric is lossless so the Q is idealized.
