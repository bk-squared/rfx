A short, visual tour of what rfx can do. Each case shows you three things:
**what the structure is**, **what the physics does** as the fields evolve, and
**how close the answer lands to the exact textbook result**. Pick one to dive in.

    A wave travels cleanly down an empty rectangular metal pipe. Almost nothing bounces back — exactly what theory predicts for an ideal guide. [See it →](/rfx/gallery/waveguide_wr90/)
  </Card>
