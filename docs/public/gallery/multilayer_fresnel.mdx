Normal-incidence reflection and transmission of a dielectric slab
(εr = 4.0, 10 mm) driven by a TFSF plane wave, mapped onto a 2-port S-matrix
(`|S11|² = R`, `|S21|² = T`).

<Aside type="tip" title="Validation — tier E5">
**Metric:** `|S11|² = R` and `|S21|² = T` vs the exact transfer-matrix solution.
**Tolerance:** mean `|R/T error| < 0.05`, energy `|R+T−1| < 0.05`.
**Reference solver:** analytic transfer matrix (Meep secondary cross-check).
</Aside>
