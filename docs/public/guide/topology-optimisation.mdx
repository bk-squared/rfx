## Overview

This guide will cover rfx's topology optimisation pipeline:

- **Density parameterisation** — continuous relaxation of material presence
- **Cone filter** — minimum feature-size enforcement via spatial filtering
- **Smooth Heaviside projection** — binarisation with differentiable thresholding
- **Beta continuation schedule** — progressive sharpening toward 0/1 designs
- **Gradient computation** — `jax.grad` through the full FDTD loop

See [Gradient Behavior](../gradient-behavior/) for where gradients are reliable and [Inverse Design](../inverse-design/) for the broader design framework.
